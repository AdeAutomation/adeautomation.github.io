Our Services


  • We offer services that improve schools in African countries, upgrading them to international standards by providing professional development for teachers and school leaders.
  • We provide models of effective instructions and support for teachers.
  • We purchase new books, supplies and technical equipment for schools and new training programs for teachers and leaders to improve instruction.
  • We hold meetings with proprietors, school managers and teachers to discuss and improve performance
  • We review and develop curriculum that meet today’s educational needs.


School Improvement

  • Developing a rigorous standard based curriculum.
  • Promote effective teaching in every classroom
  • Develop strong home, school and community partnerships.
  • Promote a culture and climate that promotes learning
  • Assist the Proprietor develop a school-wide assessment framework and organizational structures that promote effective learning.


Study Abroad

This program is designed to help students and their families seek admission into colleges abroad. Each step is personalized to help the student find the "best fit" college that will meet the needs and priorities of the students, and to demonstrate their uniqueness in the application process. We counsel students through personality and career assessments, organize and assist them in creating a timeline of the college process, develop an appropriate college list, provide specific help with course selection, applications, essays, interviews, and preparation for visas
