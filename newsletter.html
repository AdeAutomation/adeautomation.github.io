Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a classroom or lecture theatre?

From Week one of the autumn semester to the end of the spring semester, SAA look after the booking of classrooms/lecture theatres on campus. Complete the room booking form on the SAA website. It must be signed by your HOD/Line Manager. If the booking is ‘out of hours’ it will also require a signature from the Office of Buildings and Estates.

2. Whom do I contact to book a classroom or lecture theatre?

Week 1 of autumn semester to end of spring semester – contact SAA Summer time bookings – contact Accommodation@ul.ie in PCC Orientation Week – contact the Undergraduate Admissions Office

3. What are the payment method available?

At the moment, we only accept Credit/Debit cards and Paypal payments. Learn more about major banks accepting card transactions.

4. Can all rooms on campus be booked through the above channels, i,e SAA, Admissions, Accommodation?

No, a list of those available to book through these channels can be viewed at www.timetable.u.ie by clicking on “Room Details”.
