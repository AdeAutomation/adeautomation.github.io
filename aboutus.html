About Us

Leadership Team




Femi Adebanjo has over 23 years of experience of teaching Mathematics in one of the largest education system in America, New York City Dept. of Education. He is an Adjunct Professor at City University of Technology, Brooklyn, New York. Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, Femi Adebanjo attended Mayflower School, Ikenne and obtained B.Sc. Physics at University of Lagos in 1986. He had a brief employment with Ministry of Education, Abeokuta, Nigeria as Inspector of Education.His major responsibility was facilitating quality control and assessment of education policy of schools in conjunction with Principal Managers.

He migrated to the United States in 1990 with deep passion for computer science and a relentless pursuit of carrier in education. He obtained M.A. in Mathematics Education at New York University, New York and currently in pursuant of Ph.D. degree in Leadership in Educational Policy and Administration. Notable accomplishment includes being a Mathematics Coach for Intermediate School teachers for 4 years and a member of a Mathematics Curriculum Review and Development Committee charged with the task of developing mathematics curriculum that would suit both the high achieving students and at risk students so they can be as successful in their academic.

He also facilitated school-based Professional Developments on integrating technology into classroom learning and teaching techniques and best practices. He is a member of the AERA, with special interest in curriculum development and Educational Research Projects.





Adeshola Adebanjo has an impressive 20 years of experience in Information Technology. He is highly experienced, energetic and articulate in infrastructure operations and Systems Engineering and has worked as a team manager and as a consultant in various business sectors, ranging from start-ups to huge global conglomerates maintaining their core infrastructures.

Adeshola also has an unparalleled record in optimising solutions and troubleshooting issues within diverse technical environments, coordinating team efforts for optimization and problem resolution. As a team leader, he has the ablity to build strong professional relationships and communicate effectively, working in numerous enterprise environments and on projects across multiple sectors and countries, whilst also supporting business strategies, managing effective relationships with internal stakeholders and outsourced service providers.

In Systems Engineering, he has practical hands-on experience in providing specialist design, configuration, implementation, administration, migration and ongoing support of multitude of systems, be it hardware or software and more recently, implementing and maintaining innovative cloud computing solutions using Amazon Web Services (AWS).
